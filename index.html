About Us

The idea of establishing Pataleban Vineyard Resort came up with the influence of winery resort in Japan. The company was registered in 2006 in Nepal affiliated to NICHIBI KAIKAN of Tokyo, Japan. The objective of the company is to develop eco-tourism, horticulture and organic farming.

The Resort site lies 16km. west of Kathmandu city at an altitude of 1600m. above sea level. It covers an area of 2 hector facing north west at Chisapani Village, Baadbhanjyang VDC. 5, Kathmandu, Nepal. It is surrounded by a community forest known as Pataleban, have great views of sunset, the Himalayan range of Langtang, Ganesh, Manaslu, Annapurna and the Valley of Dhading.
